Banana Cake

How to prepare Banana Cake

This is a recipes for preparing Banana cake.

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups mashed bananas
  • 2 teaspoons lemon juice
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1½ teaspoons baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 2⅛ cups white sugar
  • ¾ cup butter
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1½ cups buttermilk

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 275 degrees F (135 degrees C). Grease and flour a 9x13-inch pan.

Step 2

Make cake: Mix mashed bananas with lemon juice in a small bowl. Mix flour, baking soda

and salt in a medium bowl.

Step 3

Cream sugar and butter in a large bowl until light and fluffy, 3 to 4 minutes.

Beat in eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla. Beat in flour mixture alternately with buttermilk.

Stir in banana mixture. Pour batter into the prepared pan.

Step 4

Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean, about

1 hour. Remove cake from the oven and place it directly into the freezer for 45 minutes. This will make

the cake very moist.

Waffles

How to prepare Waffles

This is a recipes for preparing Waffles.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1¾ cusps milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 4 teaspoons Baking Powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ Vanilla extract
  • nonstick cooking spray

Step 1

Preheat a waffle iron according to manufacturer's instructions.

Step 2

Whist eggs in a large bowl until light and fluffy. Add flour, milk and vegetable oil and mix to combine.

Whisk in sugar, then mix in baking powder, sald and vanilla just until smooth, being careful not to over-mix.

Step 3

Spray the preheated waffle iron with non-stick spray. Pour batter onto the hot waffle iron and cook until golden-brown

and the iron stops steaming, 3 to 5 minutes.
